About Idiog

Idiog is a Knowledge and Media Consulting firm based on action research model working in the development sector. We work across the social spectrum with Government, Corporates, Media, NGO’s and Communities. Our work extends from policy making to capacity building sessions in order to provide level playing field to all the players within the sector. Our approach through our projects is to generate, analyse and intervene with our own set of ideas and knowledge and reach out to like-minded stakeholders for implementing them successfully.
