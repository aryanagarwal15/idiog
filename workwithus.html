Work With Us



JOIN OUR TEAM AND WORK FLEXIBLY
IN GREAT ATMOSPHERE

We always look for candidates with entrepreneurial zeal to solve problems through teamwork interventions and in an individual leadership capacity. We support our team members in long run by giving them chance to lead projects, raise their concerns and full-fledged mentorship whenever they required.

Senior Consultants - We are looking for Senior Consultants with relevant experience in development sector. The ideal candidate must have an understanding of CSR, led projects and a team leader to conduct studies independently. Candidates coming from social work/development studies will be given preference. Research skills, writing and field work skills are non-negotiable.


Research Associates – We are looking for Research Associates with experiences in development research. The ideal candidate must be able to conduct secondary research, write article and support studies. Candidates with Public Policy, Social research, and Development Economics will be given preference.


Media Manager – We are looking for a manager who can manage media spaces including online as well as offline to connect with our clients, stakeholders and the team. An ideal candidate should be well equipped with required skills with experience in the media sector.


Interns - We invite motivated interns across fields to work with us in team. We also accept innovative projects from college students related to our ongoing projects.
